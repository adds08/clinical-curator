# Patient Manual

Welcome to Clinical Curator. This guide walks you through everything you can do
as a patient — viewing your health summary, managing records, requesting
services, and controlling who sees your data.

---

## Getting Started

### Create an account

1. On the login screen, tap **Create Account**.
2. Enter your full name, email, phone number, and a password of at least 8
   characters.
3. Accept the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy, then tap **Sign Up**.
4. You will land on the Patient Home screen.

### Sign in

1. Enter your registered email and password.
2. Tap **Login**. You will be taken to the screen for your role.

### Switching roles

If you are also a verified practitioner, a **Doctor View** toggle appears in
Profile. Use it to switch contexts without signing out.

## Home Screen

The Patient Home screen gives you an at-a-glance overview of your health:

- **Greeting** — a personalized welcome and a notification bell.
- **Health Summary Card** — your latest heart rate, blood pressure, and the
  time of the last reading, with a "View Details" link for full trends.
- **Profile Completion** — a progress bar prompting you to fill in emergency
  contacts, insurance, and other missing details.
- **Public Health Updates** — active alerts in your area (e.g. dengue
  warnings, vaccination drives).
- **Medical Services Grid** — quick-access tiles for Records, Hospitals,
  Telemedicine, Ambulance, Health Tips, Lab Booking, Pharmacy, and Insurance.

---

## Medical Records

Open the **Records** tab from the bottom navigation to see your full medical
history.

- **Browse** — records appear in reverse chronological order.
- **Filter** — use the chips at the top to narrow by:
  - **Labs** — blood work, urinalysis, and other laboratory results
  - **Prescriptions** — current and past medications
  - **Imaging** — X-rays, MRI, CT, and other imaging reports
  - **Immunizations** — vaccinations and upcoming schedules
- **Details** — tap any card to see provider notes, attached files, and
  history.

### Cardiovascular detail

Tapping a cardiovascular record opens a focused view with heart rate and
blood pressure trends, ECG summaries where available, and physician notes.

## Services

### Ambulance

1. From Services or the Home grid, tap **Ambulance**.
2. Fill in the request form — location, emergency type, patient condition,
   and contact phone.
3. Submit, review the confirmation, and confirm.
4. Track the ambulance in real time on the tracking screen with ETA, driver
   details, and live status.

### Telemedicine

1. Tap **Telemedicine** from Services or the Home grid.
2. Search doctors by specialty, name, or availability.
3. Book a time slot.
4. At the scheduled time, join the video call from the app — camera, mic,
   and chat controls are available in-call.

### Health Tips

Curated articles covering nutrition, exercise, mental health, disease
prevention, and seasonal guidance. No account-specific data is needed.

### Hospitals, Lab Booking, Pharmacy, Insurance

Each service tile follows the same flow — browse, select, confirm. Your
bookings and orders appear under **My Appointments** in the Booking hub.

## Consent Management

You control who can see your records. Open **Profile > Consent Management**.

### Granting access

1. Tap **Grant Access**.
2. Find the practitioner by name or scan their QR code.
3. Pick the scope — full records, specific record types, or time-limited
   access.
4. Confirm.

### Revoking access

1. Open the practitioner's entry in your active consents list.
2. Tap **Revoke Access** and confirm. The practitioner loses access
   immediately.

### Sharing via QR

1. Tap **Share via QR** to generate a QR code holding your consent token.
2. The practitioner scans it to request access.
3. You receive a notification to approve or deny the request.

## Offline Support

Clinical Curator is offline-first:

- Records you view are cached locally on your device.
- When offline, you can still browse records, view vitals, and access
  previously loaded data.
- Any data you create offline (ambulance requests, appointment bookings)
  uploads automatically when you reconnect.
- A sync indicator in the top bar shows current status. Server copies win
  on conflict for clinical records.

To pre-download everything for offline, use **Download for Offline** in
Settings.

## Profile & Settings

Open the **Profile** tab for:

- **Account** — name, email, phone, photo.
- **Language** — switch the app language.
- **Security** — change password, enable biometric login, configure 2FA.
- **Notifications** — appointment reminders, health alerts, lab results.
- **Backup & Restore** — export a local backup or back up to Google Drive.
- **Consent Management** — see the section above.
- **Role Toggle** — switch to Doctor View if you are a verified practitioner.
